About

Raci Elon is a collection of NFTs used to trade in-game items and other NFTs in the Play To Earn and Metaverse mechanics games.Users can use $RACI as governance token in Metaverse game, fight and earn items with star level from 1-5 to farm and fight to get many rewards. The game will be released in Q1 2022

Tokenomics

Name : Raci Elon

Total Supply : 1.000.000.000 RACI

Decimals : 18

We will sell all the tokens and will use part of the transaction fees of the players in the game to buy back the tokens and burn $RACI to increase the token price, in this way the people who are project token holder will enjoy the game and earn money from the Game.
